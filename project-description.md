# Healer Fantasy (name TBD)

Healer fantasy is a game heavily inspired by the party-healing style gameplay of World of Warcraft dungeons and raids, but set in a simple 2D world and played solo.

The player takes on the role of the healer, a spellcaster which they will choose spells and talents for in order to keep their party alive whilst killing bosses.

The party consists of the player and their 3 NPC companions:

- The Templar (defensive focus, heavy frontliner with some self-healing)
- The Assassin (a sneaky rogue dealing damage up close)
- The Wizard (a spellcaster dealing damage from a distance)

## Gameplay

The party will take on boss fights. During a boss fight, the healer will have to use their selected spells (affected by their talents) to:

- Keep the party alive by casting helpful spells, such as healing or shielding the party
- These can either affect the entire group, or be cast individually on party members by casting them with the mouse over that party member's frame in the UI (WoW-style)
- Help the party deal damage to the boss

The player may focus more on either of these aspects via their spell and talent selection.
